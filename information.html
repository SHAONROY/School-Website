Facilities

A) Environment: A pleasant and safe environment and a wide open field inside the campus.

B) Medium of Education: The institution is run through Bengali but special emphasis is given on English, moral education and co-educational activities.

C) Characteristics of the teacher congregation: To provide proper overall education through competent, humble, hardworking teacher / teacher congregation.

D) Co-educational activities: Girls Guide, Bangla Debate, Music, Painting, Dance, Recitation, Speech, Drama, Serial Storytelling, Qurat, General Knowledge, Volleyball, Basketball and Club activities are on.
